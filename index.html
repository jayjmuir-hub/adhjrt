A grassroots festival, run by the community

Founded as the Abu Dhabi Bats and renamed Harlequins in 2008, the Quins are a not-for-profit, volunteer-run club. This tournament is organised the same way — coaches, pitch marshals and medics are almost entirely parent volunteers giving up their weekend for the kids.

Tournament Format

Pool play on Saturday determines Sunday's bracket seeding across every division. Every team is guaranteed a minimum of four matches across the weekend, with finals played on the main pitch Sunday afternoon.

  • VenueZayed Sports City

Who Plays

Divisions

Following the Quins' own junior pathway — from first touches of the ball to full-contact youth rugby.

Minis
U6 – U8 · Tag Rugby
Non-contact, small-sided games focused on ball skills and having fun.
Midis
U9 – U11 · Contact
First introduction to contact rugby, with modified laws and pitch sizes.
Youth
U12 – U14 · Full Contact
Full-format matches building toward the club's senior pathway.
Girls
U12 – U18 · Mixed Format
Dedicated girls' divisions across age groups, contact and non-contact.
